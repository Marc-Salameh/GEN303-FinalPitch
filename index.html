About LiStick

LiStick is a cutting-edge assistive device designed to enhance safety and independence for visually impaired individuals. It combines advanced features like obstacle detection, GPS tracking, and Li-Fi communication to address key mobility challenges while promoting peace of mind for caregivers.

Features

  • Obstacle Detection with Ultrasonic Sensors
  • Real-Time GPS Tracking and Caregiver Alerts
  • Li-Fi Communication for Secure Data Transfer
  • Water-Level Detection for Hazard Prevention
  • Mobile App Integration for Seamless Monitoring

Target Market

LiStick targets visually impaired individuals aged 15–65 and elderly individuals (65+) with age-related vision loss. Our primary focus is on urban areas in Lebanon, with plans to expand across the MENA region. By addressing the needs of 70,000 visually impaired individuals in Lebanon, we aim to empower 1,750 users in the first phase, generating $175,000 in revenue.

Social and Environmental Impact

LiStick supports the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals, including:

  • Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being
  • Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities
  • Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

We prioritize eco-friendly materials, recycling programs, and sustainable manufacturing to reduce our carbon footprint.
